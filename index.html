The Flip'N Calculator


Before selling a card, it makes sense to figure out what you would need to sell it for in order to make a profit or at least break even. The boring and tedious part is doing the math to account for all the fees that Ebay and Mercari will charge you. With our Flip'N Calculator you will no longer need to do that math becuase we have done it for you!

How it works:

    In 1 simple step, the Flip'N Calculator will give you the price you need to sell your card at in order to make a profit.
  1. Enter the dollar amount you have invested in the card and press enter.

Profit Percentages

The Flip'N Calculator takes into account the following fees associated with selling through Ebay and Mercari
  • Final Value Fee (Ebay/Mercari) = 10%
  • Paypal Transaction Fee / Mercari Transaction Fee = 2.9% + $0.30
